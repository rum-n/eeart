Privacy Policy

Eastern European Art respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that use Eastern European Art, as explained in this privacy policy ("Privacy Policy").

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE Eastern European Art does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, email address, etc.) except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information. Eastern European Art collects personal information and information about your transactions when you use Eastern European Art services, such as buying and selling items. When you register, Eastern European Art asks for your email address. Additional information is requested when you buy and sell items, such as your name, address, and phone number. Once you register with Eastern European Art and sign in, you are no longer anonymous to Eastern European Art. The Eastern European Art server logs automatically receive and record information from your browser, including your IP address, Eastern European Art cookie information, and the page requested, and other standard information.

2. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE Your email address and other personal information is kept private. Eastern European Art does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or companies. Eastern European Art may divulge aggregate information about users but will not share personally identifiable information with any third party without your express consent. For example, Eastern European Art may disclose how frequently the average Eastern European Art user visits Eastern European Art, or which sort of photography or art is most popular. Eastern European Art will only release specific personal information about you if required to do so in order to comply with a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order, or if Eastern European Art believes it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of the Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law. 3. CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY Eastern European Art limits access to personal information about you to employees who Eastern European Art believes reasonably need to come into contact with that information in order to do their jobs. Eastern European Art has physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. Your Eastern European Art account information is secure, protected by password or via an email login link. 4. COOKIES Eastern European Art uses cookies to remember your shopping cart, to keep you signed in, and various tasks related to Eastern European Art. Eastern European Art will not disclose cookies to third parties except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order. 5. PREFERENCES Eastern European Art reserves the right to send you certain important communications relating to Eastern European Art, such as service announcements and administrative messages, which are considered critical to Eastern European Art membership, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them. 6. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY By using Eastern European Art, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy and to the Terms of Service. Eastern European Art may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version of the Privacy Policy can always be found at: https://www.Eastern European Art.com/privacy.aspx
